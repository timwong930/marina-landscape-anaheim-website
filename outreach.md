# Outreach

## Initial contact
- **Opener:** Hi, I’m Tim — I took a quick look at Marina Landscape - Anaheim and put together a simple idea for how the website could turn more visitors into calls or quote requests.
- **What to mention:**
  - Lead with curb appeal, recurring maintenance, and clearer service-area messaging.
  - Mention that landscaping customers respond well to strong project photos and a simple estimate path.
- **Ask:** Offer a quick visual audit showing how the site could feel more polished on a phone.
- **Tone:** Friendly, local, no-pressure, and short. Keep it practical and specific.
