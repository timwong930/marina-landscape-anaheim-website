# Marina Landscape - Anaheim website

A simple static starter site for **Marina Landscape - Anaheim** in Anaheim, CA.

## Business summary
- **Location:** Anaheim, CA
- **Public website:** https://www.marinaco.com/contact-us/locations/
- **What they do:** Landscaping

## Copy direction used in this repo
- Lead with the city, service type, and a clear next step.
- Keep the tone local, practical, and easy to scan on mobile.
- Use only public facts visible from the listing or current public presence.
- Avoid claims about reviews, licensing, years in business, or certifications unless they are already public.

## Why this version exists
- The public site looks more like a locations page than a local service page, so this starter pushes Anaheim-specific service messaging and quote flow.

## Page structure
- Hero with location, service summary, and clear call-to-action buttons
- Starter-site concept panel that explains the positioning
- Service cards with plain-English descriptions
- Service area section
- FAQ for common buyer questions
- Contact section with a simple next step
